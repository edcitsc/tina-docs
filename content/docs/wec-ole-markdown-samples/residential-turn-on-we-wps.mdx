# Description

A Turn On order is issued to initiate billing for a new or existing customer. This request can only be accepted from the customer of record.

> Note: Joint Metering accounts are identified with `HMTR` or `HSE MTR` in the Address Modifier field and must remain in the property owner's name. Refer to [Joint Metering Investigation](https://ole.wecenergygroup.com/sites/OLE/OLEV2/Pages/joint-metering-investigation.aspx) for WPS or [Joint Metering Investigation WE](https://ole.wecenergygroup.com/sites/OLE/OLEV2/Pages/Joint-Metering-Investigation-WE.aspx) for WE.

> Note: If customer of record or third party requests to put service in the name of a revocable trust or irrevocable trust, refer to [Customer Confidentiality](https://ole.wecenergygroup.com/sites/OLE/OLEV2/Pages/Customer-Confidentiality.aspx).

> Note: If customer mentions the customer of record is deceased, refer to [Customer of Record is Deceased](https://ole.wecenergygroup.com/sites/OLE/OLEV2/Pages/Customer-of-Record-is-Deceased.aspx).

> Note: WE only. The following are examples of situations handled by the Small Business Center (SBC): notification of purchase or sale of apartment building(s) with 6 or more units; requests for major changes to an owner agreement such as adding a new apartment building or complex or changing an existing owner agreement type; and stop or start service orders involving sale of property with 6 or more units when the caller has new owner information. When completing stop or start orders for customers who have been aligned by SBC, access the address given by the customer, find active accounts for that address, choose the owner agreement, and choose the customer application associated with that owner agreement.

> Note: WPS only. There is no limit to the number of accounts that can be handled on a call. It is the goal to resolve all customer inquiries during the first contact. However, if the customer has six or more [Turn On](https://ole.wecenergygroup.com/sites/OLE/OLEV2/Pages/OleV2-Index.aspx?guid=fedc1c66-d29c-4f51-b3b7-9a8d93d941ff\&pn=Start%20Service) or [Turn Off](https://ole.wecenergygroup.com/sites/OLE/OLEV2/Pages/Turn%20Off.aspx) requests, advise the customer that the process time for the requests will be lengthy. Offer the option of using the company's website or e-mail. Refer to [Handle Multiple Accounts](https://ole.wecenergygroup.com/sites/OLE/OLEV2/Pages/Handle-Multiple-Accounts.aspx).

# Turn On Questions and Answers

## Can a Turn On request be created by anyone other than customer of record?

No. Only customer of record or related contract customer (Y, Y) can request a Turn On. Refer to [Customer Confidentiality](https://ole.wecenergygroup.com/sites/OLE/OLEV2/Pages/Customer-Confidentiality.aspx).

## If all services are not selected when issuing a Turn On order, what happens to the unselected services?

The unselected services remain in the previous customer's name.

> Example: A yard light will continue to bill to the previous customer.

## Realtor or third party calls requesting unseal for home inspection and sealed once inspection is complete.

Inform caller to have customer of record contact us to make the request.

If the property is in foreclosure, refer to [Foreclosure Information](https://ole.wecenergygroup.com/sites/OLE/OLEV2/Pages/Foreclosure-Information.aspx).

## What does it mean when an error message displays stating services need to be configured while doing a Turn On for a new landlord with an offering of Small Coml & Ind Gas?

Modify the Power Billing contract attribute to Yes. Rental properties that provide service for more than one unit will display this error message.

## Can an owner agreement be set up by information provided by the tenant?

No. Owner agreements must be set up directly with the landlord.

## If a customer record exists but the customer is requesting service for a different utility, can the same record be used?

No. A new customer record must be created as part of the Turn On.

Examples:

* Customer record exists for WPS and customer is requesting service for UMERC (WPS), and vice versa.
* Customer record exists for NSG and customer is requesting service for PGL, and vice versa.
* Customer record exists for MERC and customer is requesting service for MGU, and vice versa.
* Customer record exists for UMERC (WE) and customer is requesting service for UMERC (WPS), and vice versa.
* Customer record exists for WE and customer is requesting service for WPS, and vice versa.

When the new customer record is created, enter a comment indicating the utility for which the record was created in the Alert attribute field.

> Example: "CUST # for UMERC accounts."

## If electric service is turned off via RSS, at what time will the Turn On order complete and service turn on?

* Same-day orders submitted 12 a.m. - 8 a.m.: signal is sent after 8 a.m.
* Same-day orders submitted 8 a.m. - 11:59 p.m.: signal is sent immediately.
* Future-date orders: signal is sent after 8 a.m. on the Wanted Date.

# RSS Reconnect Troubleshooting

## Customer calls back to report the electric service was not turned on as expected.

1. [Locate a Contract](https://ole.wecenergygroup.com/sites/OLE/OLEV2/Pages/locate-a-contract.aspx).
2. To verify the order status and determine whether RSS failed:
   * On the Orders tab, select the Turn On order, then select History.
   * The Order Action History window displays.
   * View the Reason field.
3. If the reason is one of the following:
   * `RSS Command Failure`
   * `RSS Command Reading Failure`
   * `RSS Results Unknown`
   Then:
   * Confirm with the customer that the main breaker is shut off.
   * Contact Support Line if available, or Team Leader Floor Support if Support Line is not available.

> Note: Support Line or the team leader retries the connection attempt.

1. If the reason is `RSS Dispatch` and the Completion Field Action line lists RSS as the user ID:
   * Ask the customer to verify that the main breaker has been turned back on or the main fuse block reinstalled.
   * In a multi-unit or commercial building, the main breaker may be located next to the meter and the meter may be outside.

## Leaders or Support Line only: Customer contacts the Care Center indicating service was not reconnected.

> Note: Use when RSS reconnect failure occurs 4:00 p.m. - 8:00 a.m. Monday - Sunday.

1. Access Command Center to attempt a second reconnect.
2. If the utility is WE:
   * 8 a.m. - 4 p.m. Monday through Friday: the order automatically routes to PCAD. Field Services dispatches and works these orders.
   * 4 p.m. - 10:30 p.m. Monday through Friday and Saturday 8 a.m. - 5 p.m.: Support Line or Care Center Team Leader Floor Support resends the reconnect signal.
   * 10:30 p.m. - 8 a.m. Monday through Sunday: third shift consultants access Command Center and resend the signal.
   * If the order fails again, the order waits until Field Service dispatch reviews it the next business day and dispatches the order to reconnect the meter because of failed RSS reconnection.
   * Send second signal to reconnect service.
   * If the second reconnect attempt fails after 4 p.m., advise the consultant to tell the customer a Field Service employee will reconnect the next business day after 8 a.m. Monday through Friday.
   * Medical reconnects or company errors after hours only: advise the consultant to issue an Electric Trouble reconnect order with:
     * Order Category: `Meter Orders`
   * Order Type: `Misc Meter Order`
     * Select requestor type from Reason.
   * Check Electric Service Type.
     * Wanted Date defaults to today.
   * Completion Method: `Normal Completion`
     * On the Scheduling tab, Miscellaneous Order Work Type - WE: `Temp Service Restore`
3. If the utility is WPS:
   * During business hours Monday through Friday, the order automatically routes to paper. The Local Office dispatches and works these orders.
   * After local office business hours Monday through Friday and Saturday, Support Line or Care Center Team Leader Floor Support resends the reconnect signal.
   * 10:30 p.m. - 8 a.m. Monday through Sunday, third shift consultants access Command Center and resend the signal.
   * If the order fails again, the order waits until the Local Office dispatcher reviews it the next business day and dispatches the order to reconnect the meter because of failed RSS reconnection.
   * Send second signal to reconnect service.
   * If the second reconnect attempt fails after 4:00 p.m., advise the consultant to tell the customer a Field Service employee will reconnect the next business day after 8 a.m. Monday through Friday.
   * Medical reconnects or company errors after hours: create an Electric Trouble order with:
     * Order Category: `Electric Trouble Orders`
   * Order Type: `Power Not Out`
     * Select requestor type from Reason.
   * Check Electric Service Type.
     * In Instructions enter: `RSS Turn On failed. Emergency reconnection needed.`
