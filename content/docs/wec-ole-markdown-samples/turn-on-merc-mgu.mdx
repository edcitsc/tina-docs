# Description

A Turn On order is issued to initiate billing for a new or existing customer. This request can only be accepted by the customer of record.

> Note: If customer of record or third party requests to put service in the name of a revocable trust or irrevocable trust, refer to [Customer Confidentiality](//OLECustomer-Confidentiality.aspx).

> Note: If customers request the status of a scheduled appointment or missed appointment, refer to [Verifiy Appointment Status](//OLEVerify-Appointment-Status.aspx).

> Note: If customer mentions the customer of record is deceased, refer to [Customer of Record is Deceased](//OLECustomer-of-Record-is-Deceased.aspx).

# Turn On Frequently Asked Questions

## Can a Turn On request be created by anyone other than customer of record?

No. Only customer of record or related contract customer (Y, Y) can request a Turn On. Refer to [Customer Confidentiality](//OLECustomer-Confidentiality.aspx).

## If Wanted Date for an in-progress Turn Off/On is changed to a date more than 2 days from the Wanted Date, will the status change to pending?

No. The status remains in progress and the field order reissues when it is within 2 days of the new Wanted Date.

## If all services are not selected when issuing a Turn On order, what happens to the unselected services?

The unselected services remain in the previous customer's name.

> Example: MERC ServiceChoice continues to bill to the previous customer.

## Realtor or third party calls requesting unseal for home inspection and sealed once inspection is complete.

Inform caller to have customer of record contact us to make the request.

## How do I make the premise service active if I cancelled a prior reading and did not select the Active button?

Premise Services are active when a turn on is completed and inactive without a turn on. Reissue the Turn On with a completion method of Complete Now Without Dispatch and then complete it. The service then becomes active.

## What does it mean when an error message states services need to be configured while doing a turn on for a new landlord with an offering of Small Coml & Ind Gas?

Modify the Power Billing contract attribute to Yes. Rental properties that provide service for more than one unit display this error message.

## There is an In Progress turn on or turn off order and now the customer is requesting the meters either sealed or unsealed.

If an in-progress order is pending and the customer now wants the meter either sealed or unsealed, create a note follow-up to the back office and include the seal or unseal request in remarks.

## Can an owner agreement be set up by information provided by the tenant?

No. Owner agreements must be set up directly with the landlord.

## If a customer record exists but the customer is requesting service for a different utility, can the same record be used?

No. A new customer record must be created as part of the Turn On.

Examples:

* Customer record exists for WPS and customer is requesting service for UMERC (WPS) and vice versa.
* Customer record exists for NSG and customer is requesting service for PGL and vice versa.
* Customer record exists for MERC and customer is requesting service for MGU and vice versa.
* Customer record exists for UMERC (WE) and customer is requesting service for UMERC (WPS) and vice versa.
* Customer record exists for WE and customer is requesting service for WPS and vice versa.

When the new customer record is created, enter a comment indicating the utility for which the record was created in the Alert attribute field.

> Example: "CUST # for UMERC accounts."

## Do technicians carry ladders to light overhead and/or rooftop heating units?

* MERC only: Technicians do not carry ladders but will use them to light overhead heating units.
  * Ladders must be 6 feet or less and require three points of contact at all times.
  * Note: If higher than six feet, special arrangements must be made.
  * Technicians do not light rooftop heating units.
* MGU only: Technicians do not light overhead heating units.
  * Technicians do not carry ladders and will not use customer-provided ladders.

## 5. Determine if meters need to be unsealed.

1. Review the premise to determine whether meters need to be unsealed.
2. If Yes, advise the customer of the following:
   * We must schedule an appointment to gain access to the gas meter and appliances in order to unseal the gas meter, relight pilots, and complete required inspections.
   * Verify the electric meter is active at the residence. Gas Mechanics cannot unseal other electric utility company meters and need electric utility active to relight appliances and perform safety inspections.
   * Verify meter location. Refer to [View a Premise](//OLEView-a-Premise.aspx).
3. If the meter is on the roof, advise:
   * MERC only: Technicians do not carry ladders but will use them to light overhead heating units.
     * Ladder must be 6 feet or less and requires three points of contact at all times.
   * Note: If higher than six feet, special arrangements must be made.
     * Technicians do not light rooftop heating units or access the roof to turn on the meter. An HVAC must be present.
   * MGU only: Technicians do not light overhead heating units.
     * Technicians do not carry ladders and will not use customer-provided ladders.
   * Technicians do not access the roof to turn on the meter. An HVAC must be present.
4. Access requirements:
   * MGU only: Requires someone 18 years of age or older to provide access to the property.
   * MERC only: Someone 18 years of age or older must provide access to the property, or one of the following must be available:
     * House left unlocked
   * Key left for technician
     * Meet a neighbor
   * Get a key from a neighbor
     * Garage door keyless entry code
5. If the customer who requested meters sealed is requesting unseal within 12 months, reconnection charges are assessed by Billing for each meter. MERC only: customer charges are back billed for the months the meter was sealed.
6. If meters need unsealed:
   * MERC only: Proceed to Step 6.
   * MGU only: Proceed to Step 7.
7. If meters do not need unsealed:
   * MERC only: Proceed to Step 6.
   * MGU only: Proceed to Step 7.
